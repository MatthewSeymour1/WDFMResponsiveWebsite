How to sell surplus food with Savour

You can sell unsold meals, fresh produce, baked goods, or any surplus food that meets local health and safety regulations. Our app is designed to help you maximize your impact and minimize waste.

Sign up for a business account, list your surplus items, and set your prices. Savour handles the rest, connecting you with customers who are ready to buy!

Not only will you help reduce waste and contribute to sustainability, but you'll also attract eco-conscious customers and unlock a new source of income. It’s a win for your business and the planet.
